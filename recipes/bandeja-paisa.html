Bandeja Paisa

Description

The most complete meal, the most delicious meal. La Bandeja Paisa comes straight from Antioquia, Colombia, a region known for its coffee, hospitality, music (Maluma, JBalvin, Karol G), beautiful women, and much more!

This platter has it all: rice, beans, beef, eggs, fried pork belly (chicharrón) , fried plantains, blood sausage (morcilla), chorizo, arepa, and some avocado. You will be full once the plat is clean, but you will definitely ask for more! Let's get into the recipe:

Ingredients (one serving)

  • 1 Cup of White Rice
  • 1 Cup of Pinto Beans
  • 1 Cup of Powdered Beef
  • 1 Fried Pork Belly
  • 1 Cooked Chorizo
  • 1 Fried Blood Sausage
  • 1 Fried Egg
  • 1 Fried Plantains
  • 1 Arepa
  • 1/2 of an Avocado
  1. Prepare the white rice, beans, and powdered beef.
  2. Fry the egg, plantain, blood sausage, pork belly, and chorizo.
  3. Either buy the arepa already made or make the arepa on a grill.
  4. Assemble all the ingredients together and add the avocado as the finishing touch.
