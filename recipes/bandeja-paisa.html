Bandeja Paisa

Enjoy a Taste of Colombia!

The most complete meal, the most delicious meal. La Bandeja Paisa comes straight from Antioquia, Colombia, a region known for its coffee, hospitality, music (Maluma, JBalvin, Karol G), beautiful women, and much more!
